# Seager's Fruit & Veg — Business & Family Info

## Business Details
- **Business name**: Seager's Fruit & Veg / Seagers Farm / Seager Strawberry Farm Shop
- **Address**: Wells Road, Draycott, near Cheddar, Somerset BS27 3ST
- **Phone**: 01749 870519
- **Hours**: Mon–Sun 8:00–17:00 (open 7 days, including bank holidays)
- **Location note**: On the A371 between Draycott and Rodney Stoke
- **Facebook**: https://www.facebook.com/pages/Strawberry%20Farm%20Shop/634138796635413/

## What They Sell
- Strawberries (famous Cheddar Valley strawberries, grown in polytunnels metres from the stall)
- Seasonal fruit & veg (all year round)
- Pumpkins (autumn)
- Christmas trees (festive season)

## Farm Infrastructure
- 125 metres of polytunnels filled with strawberry plants
- Around 29,000 strawberry plants total (15,000 in greenhouse, 14,000 across polytunnels/open tunnels)
- Peak season: May–June, roughly 100 trays picked daily
- Bees used for natural pollination
- Strawberries handled only once — when picked — sold same day

## Family Tree
- **Margaret Seagers** (grandmother) — started the business in the 1940s with a roadside stall on Wells Road while her husband Ted served in WWII
- **Ted Seagers** (grandfather) — served as a mechanic in the Second World War
- **Andrew Seagers** — Margaret's son, left school at 14 to expand the farm
- **Chris Seager** — current owner, third generation, runs the farm
- **Lenny Seager** — Chris's son, helps out on the farm (fourth generation)

## Local Context
- **The Strawberry Line**: Former Cheddar Valley Railway, nicknamed "The Strawberry Line" for carrying strawberries to London markets. Now a 10-mile walking/cycling path from Cheddar to Yatton (plans to extend Shepton Mallet to Clevedon)
  - https://www.thestrawberryline.org.uk/
- **Draycott Strawberry Fayre**: Annual village event since the 1970s, held 2nd weekend of June
  - http://www.draycottstrawberryfayre.co.uk/
- The Cheddar Valley has been famous for strawberries since the 1800s, grown on south-facing slopes of the Mendip Hills
